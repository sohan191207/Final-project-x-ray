Frequently Asked Questions
Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

How do hospitals upload X-ray images?

Hospitals can easily upload patient information and X-ray images using their secure dashboard after logging in.

Can doctors work from anywhere?

Yes. Verified doctors can access their dashboard, review cases, and submit reports remotely — no fixed hospital contract needed.

Is patient data secure on this platform?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted and accessible only to authorized users, ensuring full privacy and compliance with medical standards.

How do hospitals assign reports to doctors

Hospitals can choose a doctor from a verified list or let the system assign a doctor automatically based on availability.

How do doctors get paid for their reports?

Doctors receive payments directly through their wallet on the platform and can withdraw funds anytime after meeting minimum requirements.

Can patients access their reports directly?

Currently, hospitals are responsible for delivering completed reports to patients, but patient access features are planned for future updates.
